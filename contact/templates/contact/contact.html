Enquiries

Club Enquiries:

For enquiries about existing table tennis clubs:

Please see the contacts details on the clubs page and contact the club directly.

To register a new table tennis club:

  1. Register a personal account on the website (on the signup page).
  2. Contact the league secretary (e.g. using the form below) to arrange for a visit. This visit is simply to ensure that the club exists and that the conditions meet the standards for the league (if appropriate).
  3. Following the visit, the league secretary will register your club on the website and assign you as the club administrator. You will then be able to add details about the club which (once approved) will display on the clubs page of this website.

NOTE: All clubs with teams registered in the league must have information on the clubs page, especially venue information so that other teams know how to find you.

League Enquiries:

To register a team in the league:

Please contact your club administrator who has responsibility for overseeing the league teams in your club. They will register the team on your behalf.

NOTE: All teams must be linked with a club. If you want to register a new club, please follow the instructions shown above.

To submit a match result:

Please email a picture of the match score card (signed by both team captains) to the league secretary on the address stated at the top of this page.

Other Enquiries:

For all other enquiries relating to the league or website, please contact the league secretary using the form below or the contact details provided.
